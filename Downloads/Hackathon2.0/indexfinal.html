Do you have an idea that could take the world way ahead of its time? Are you looking for the means to get the prototype of such an idea off the ground? Look no further.

UPES-CSI is providing yet another opportunity for all the coders to rise and shine.Exercise your fingers because this February, the much-anticipated Hackathon is back.UPES-CSI proudly announces Hackathon 2.0! With an event time of 48 hours, prizes worth a whooping 50k and intern from splashBI and Data Couch, Hackathon 2.0 provides double the opportunity for all the programmers, web developers, hackers out there.

Register, participate and stand a chance to earn an internship opportunity or get your work incubated by pioneers!

Registration Fee


Registration Fee: INR 800(Non-CSI), 600(Mixed team), 400(CSI)

  • All participants are expected to register & pay individually, irrespective of whether they choose to participate in a team or alone.
  • After successful registration, participants will receive a unique Participant ID by email.
  • Once all members of a team have registered and paid, they may inform the organisers about the team atleast day before of the Hackathon.
Next

Rules and Regulations

  • A team can have a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 4 members.
  • The hackathon is open to everyone - college students and graduates alike.
  • Requirements: Valid ID Proof, laptops, chargers and other hardware equipments required to complete your hack.
  • Judging criteria: Originality of the idea, design, functionality and implementation of the idea in a real time scenario. Judging is based on the software only (web/mobile app and software hack).
  • Deadline for registrations and payments - 11:59 AM, 13th Feb 2018
